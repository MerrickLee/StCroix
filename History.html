History

St. Croix Tree Service has been operating since 1974. Our first work consisted of diseased elm removal for municipal and residential clients.

In over 35 years we have seen the tremendous growth of the tree care industry, as well as our company. The affiliations we maintain with national and state tree organizations have allowed us to remain an industry leader. St. Croix Tree Service is continually working to improve customer satisfaction through on-going educational programs and weekly safety/training meetings for our employees.

St. Croix Tree Service has been a Tree Care Industry Association (TCIA) accredited company since 2005. TCIA accreditation is a voluntary program that credentials companies which meet strict criteria for compliance with industry standards, ethical business conduct, formal training programs for employees, and professionalism. The accreditation programs foundation is ethics, quality, and consumer confidence.
