About St. Croix Tree Service

St. Croix Tree Service has provided high quality tree care in East Metro and Western WI neighborhoods since 1974. Our mission is to provide aesthetically pleasing and environmentally responsible tree care to our customers and a safe, secure working environment for our employees. With our passion for excellence we aspire to make the world better one tree at a time.

Our well-trained professional crews use up to date information and modern equipment to help beautify your home and keep your family and property safe. For a free, no obligation estimate, please phone or click on the link below.

All St. Croix Tree Service sales representatives are ISA Certified and have a combined 88 years of green industry experience. We are licensed, insured, certified and accredited for your protection. Please see our testimonial page for a small sampling of our long list of satisfied customers. We would love to help you with your tree care needs as well.
