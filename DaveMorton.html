Dave Morton

Tree Planting Specialist Joined Company in January 2010davem@stcroixtreeservice.com 715-749-3475  

I have been active in the tree and landscape business for thirty years. My career began with a sales job for a hardware/gardening wholesaler out of Milwaukee, Wisconsin. In 1997, my wife took a transfer for her job and we moved to Hudson, WI where she is from. With the move, I relocated to Minnesota Valley Nursery and Landscaping in Shakopee, MN as the retail general manager, growing and selling trees and shrubs.

I have had the opportunity to run several nursery and landscape businesses over the past years. In 2010, I joined St Croix Tree Service in the tree planting and landscape division. The management is exceptional and their dedication to customer service is exceptional. I am truly interested in meeting clients, listening to their needs and coming up with a plan that will fit into their budgets.
