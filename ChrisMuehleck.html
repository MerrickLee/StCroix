Chris Muehleck

Vice President of Sales and Operations

ISA Certified Arborist BS Urban Forestry U of MN Joined Company in March of 2003 cmuehleck@stcroixtreeservice.com 651-770-3744  

I am a graduate of the U of MN Twin Cities with a BS in Urban Forestry and a minor in Forest Resource Management. I joined St. Croix Tree Service in March of 2003, when the company was much smaller and I worked with all of our Minnesota clients in assessing and consulting on their tree care needs. As the Company has grown since then, I've moved into a management role coordinating the efforts of our sales and operations management team. I feel extremely fortunate to work with such a great group of people at our successful company. While the properties of many of the customers I originally worked with are now managed by our team of arborists, I still work directly with all of our customers in and around the Stillwater area. The relationships I have with my customers is and always has been the most enjoyable part of my job for me. I get to meet and help new and interesting people every day!

I live North of Stillwater in May Township with my wife Sarah and 2 young boys Jonathan and Lincoln.
