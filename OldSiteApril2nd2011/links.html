Tree Care Industry Association Member: The Tree Care Industry Association is the professional association for tree care companies. Minnesota Society of Arboriculture: MSA is an organization that fosters an appreciation for and awareness of the urban tree community to stimulate an interest in the planning and preservation of shade and ornamental trees.
