I worked in the tree care industry for another local company while in college and achieved ISA certification in 1999 while still a student. I have been very involved in urban forestry and arboriculture since I chose it as my profession.

I live in St. Paul, in the western Macalester - Groveland neighborhood where I enjoy working with my neighbors to take the best care possible of their trees.

I have been with St. Croix Tree Service since March 2003 and have been a Sales Representative handling the majority of our Minnesota clients and ensuring that their tree care needs are always met.
