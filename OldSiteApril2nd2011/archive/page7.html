I joined St. Croix Tree Service in 1993 after earning my B.S. Degree in Urban Forestry from U.W. Stevens Point. The desire to work with trees on an individual basis and my love for everything outdoors and working with heavy equipment drew me into this field.

With St. Croix Tree Service, I became an ISA Certified Arborist and worked in almost every area of the company from planting to pruning and removal of trees. Operating our tree spades became my main duty. I enjoyed the challenges and the independent work.

In 2004 I started my own tree transplanting business and operated it for two years. I merged back with St. Croix Tree Service in September of 2006 and I am glad to be back.

My duties with SCTS now include sales, setup, and operation for our tree spade division. Installing trees from two feet tall up to twenty feet tall, making a property come to life.

I look forward to working with you on your tree planting projects.  We have a great selection of trees with numerous species and site options available.
