Rob Juetten, MN Tree Care Crew Leader
ISA Certified Arborist
Joined Co. in July of 2001

Hi! My name is Rob Juetten. I am a die hard Viking fan although I've only lived in Wisconsin and Florida. My wife of 3 years tells me no one is perfect!!! I tend to disagree.

I started working with St. Croix Tree Service in July of 2001.  I became a Certified Arborist in 2006.  Being a Certified Arborist is an honor and a privilege.  I feel like my job is like a "doctor". A good "doctor" is knowledgeable, friendly, and is passionate about their career.

Climbing trees as a child was fun and exciting and being able to do that everyday makes me feel like a kid at heart. Anytime you wake up in the morning and can't wait to have a new experience on the job is one of the best feelings in the world.

Trees are one of the most important resources and I hope to teach my children those same values.  I look
forward to using my knowledge and continuos education to improve our urban landscape for our customers for generations to come.
