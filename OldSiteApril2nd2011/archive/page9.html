Mark Gondreau, WI Tree Care Crew Leader
ISA Certified Arborist
Certified Tree Care Safety Professional
BS Urban Forestry UWSP
Joined Co. in March of 1995

I grew up in Janesville, which is in southern Wisconsin. While going to high school, I worked for the city's parks department. It was that love of working outdoors that helped me decide on a major in college. I attended the university of Wisconsin - Stevens Point, obtaining a Bachelor of Science Degree in Urban Forestry in 1994.  Those summers between class were spent working for the city's forestry department. After graduation I was hired on here at St. Croix Tree Service. I am a certified arborist, and recently became a Certified Tree Care Safety Professional.

2007 is my twelfth year with SCTS. In my time here, I have had field experience with nearly all the services we provide. I am proud to be part of our company's continued growth and dedication to proper tree care for our customers.
