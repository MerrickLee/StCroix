The logging industry was my official start into the world of trees in the early 80's.

In 1990 I started working with St. Croix Tree Service as ground support. I've been fortunate to work with many good teachers in the field. The growth of the company since I started has given me opportunities and experience in different divisions. I've earned my ISA certification along the way and have worked my way into the position of Wisconsin Tree Care Specialist as a sales representative.

Working with many of the same customers and properties over the years and developing trusting relationships is a fulfilling part of my job.
