I joined St. Croix Tree Service in 1993 after earning my BS Degree in Urban Forestry from the University of Wisconsin - Stevens Point. The desire to work with trees on an individual basis, along with my love for everything outdoors and working with heavy equipment drew me into this field.

With St. Croix Tree Service, I became an ISA Certified Arborist and worked in almost every area of the company from planting to pruning and removal of trees. Operating our tree spade became my main duty. I enjoyed the challenges and the independent work.

My duties with SCTS now include sales, setup, and operation for our tree spade division, where I install trees from two feet tall up to twenty feet tall, making a property come to life.

I look forward to working with you on your tree planting projects. We have a great selection of trees with numerous species and site options available.
