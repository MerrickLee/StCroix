Plant Health Care

It is a view like this one (or working environment in our case) that makes Plant Health Care so important to our urban environments. At St. Croix Tree Service, we view plant health care as a holistic approach to plant health management. Our PHC program provides our customers with a cost effective plant management system.

Services of the PHC program include:

  • Site inspections and problem diagnosis
  • Soil systemic insecticides
    • insect control with no spraying or trunk wounding: birch leafminer, aphids, mites, etc.
  • Spray programs - preventative disease treatments
    • apple scab, hawthorn apple rust, etc.
  • Trunk injections - dutch elm disease, oak wilt, and boring insects
  • Site Improvement / enhancement
    • specialized fertilizer mixes
    • correction of nutrient deficiencies
    • beneficial mycorrhizal bacteria
    • mulching
  • Preventative planning to reduce damage during construction process
  • Air Spade - Dig it safely and easily
