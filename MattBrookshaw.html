Matt Brookshaw

ISA Certified ArboristJoined Company in May of 2007 mbrookshaw@stcroixtreeservice.com 651-770-3744  

I was introduced to St. Croix Tree Service while still in college at UW-River Falls. I would frequently see the trucks and grew curious about the tree care industry. I started as summer help in May of 2007 an dcontinued on a part-time basis while I finished college. After earning a Bachelor's Degree in Horticulture in December 2007, I was invited to become a full-time crew member at St. Croic Tree Service.

Since I started at St. Croix Tree Service, I have gained experience in many aspects of the tree care industry ranging from plant health care to large removals and have had opportunities to learn from some of the most skilled arborists in the industry. I became an ISA Certified ARborist in 2012. In the spring of 2013, I was offered the position of a Tree Care Specialist as a Sales Representave. I am now serving many ares of the Eastern metro with all tree care needs clients may have.
