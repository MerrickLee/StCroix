Stump Removal

St. Croix Tree Service can turn any sized stump into mulch which is left on site for you to reuse. Different sized stump grinders allow us to reach stumps in almost all locations. If the homeowner is not interested in keeping the mulched stump debris or does not want to dispose of it themselves, St Croix Tree Service can remove it and completely restore the site with new top soil and grass seed.
